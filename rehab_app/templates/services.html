Treatment Programs

At Rehab Center, we offer a range of treatment programs tailored to meet the individual needs of our clients.

  • Inpatient Treatment
  • Outpatient Programs
  • Detox Services
  • Therapy Sessions

We also provide specialized programs for specific addictions such as alcoholism, drug addiction, and dual diagnosis. Our programs are designed to address the unique needs of various demographics, including adolescents, adults, and seniors.

Therapy Types

Our therapy services encompass individual, group, and family therapy sessions.

  • Individual Therapy: Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), etc.
  • Group Therapy: Support groups, psychoeducational groups, etc.
  • Family Therapy: Family counseling, communication skills training, etc.

Individual therapy provides personalized support and focuses on addressing specific issues and goals. Group therapy fosters peer support and provides opportunities for shared experiences and learning. Family therapy helps improve communication and relationships within the family system.
